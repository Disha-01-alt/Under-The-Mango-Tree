Leadership Team

Our leadership team brings together decades of experience in education, technology, and career development.

Dr. Sarah Johnson
Founder & Director

With over 15 years in education, Dr. Johnson established UTMT to make quality education accessible to all. She holds a Ph.D. in Educational Technology from Stanford University and has published numerous papers on innovative learning methodologies.

Prof. James Anderson
Lead Python Instructor

A seasoned programmer with 12 years of industry experience at top tech companies, Prof. Anderson now dedicates his time to teaching coding skills to the next generation. He specializes in Python programming and data science applications.

Ms. Emily Wong
English Department Head

With a background in HR and recruitment at Fortune 500 companies, Mr. Okoye leads our job portal and career services. He has helped hundreds of students connect with employment opportunities that match their skills and career goals.

Dr. Michael Chen

Dr. Chen oversees the technological infrastructure of UTMT's online learning platform. With a Ph.D. in Computer Science and previous experience at leading tech companies, he ensures our digital learning experience is seamless and effective.

Ms. Aisha Patel
Student Success Coordinator
